Entrepreneurship Development Programme

Learning

Entrepreneurship Development Programme is a programme meant to develop business and entrepreneurial abilities among the people. In other words, it refers to inculcation, development, enhancing and improving of entrepreneurial skills into youths who need to establish and successfully an enterprise. Thus, the concept of entrepreneurship development programme involves equipping a person with the required skills and knowledge needed for starting and running the enterprise.

Team Work

Business enterprises was started by three schools under our school entrepreneurs programs and MNPS won the best enterprise award from TATA STEEL Excellence and also the British Council Award and were rewarded with a trip to London for the same. Under this programme, MNPS started composting and our selling it to Bageecha, while JHTS have started mushroom cultivation.This project is also being run in a college in Jamshedpur on a pilot basis.
